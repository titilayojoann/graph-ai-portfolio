Why it stands out

University‑backed, hands‑on, and focused on building real AI workflows for research and work.

Module 1 — Websites, Docs & Presentations

LLM basics, generating Markdown/LaTeX/HTML, building simple sites with no‑code tools, and presentation design.

Module 2 — AI‑Automated Data Analysis

Code Interpreter, Julius, dashboards, dataset processing, automated visuals and reports, spreadsheet automations.

Module 3 — GenAI for Research & Text

Literature search (Elicit/LiteRev), text classification, sentiment analysis, knowledge maps, summarization systems.

Module 4 — Custom GPTs & Chatbots

Chat architecture, context windows & RAG, Botpress bots, integrating knowledge bases.

Module 5 — AI for Task Automation

Agent‑based flows, email response automations, browser control agents.

Module 6 — Communication & Outreach

AI‑generated summaries, scientific figures, video abstracts, visualization prompts, content scheduling.

Module 7 — Capstone & Showcase

Progress checks, peer feedback, final project presentation, public Q&A.
